Top Three Activities To Do in Toronto

Catch a Game or Show

See the Toronto Raptors, the Maple Leafs or the Blue Jays play. You could also see many shows on broadway every night.

Eat Amazing Food

You will find many cultural dishes from all over the world as well as some Canadian favorties like our poutine in the city.

View the Art and Architecture

Toronto is filled with stunning art and architecture. You can view the city's art inside and outside it's buildings.
